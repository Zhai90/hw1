Dad's S-T-E-A-L

S - Speech


What does the character say?


Dad's always tired mentally and doesn't say much throughout the story. The moment in which he says the most is when he's either having a mental breakdown or mad at absolutely everything. In the story, his speech is usually either full of furiousness, madness, regret, or sometimes, just kindness, although that last one only appears very late into the story.

T - Thoughts


What is revealed through the character's thoughts and feelings?


He suffers a lot in the early and middle part of the story, which is inevitable. It is extremely easy to notice his pain, suffering, disgrace, misery and grief in those periods of the story, although later in the story, he has obviously changed, and by a lot, almost completely reversing his attitude.

E - Effects


What is revealed through the character's effect on others?


He always tried to move on with life but fails, tries to let everyone know that he really misses Rose, to a point that the people around him don't really want to talk to him anymore, but it is really obvious that he is always trying his best.

A - Actions


What does the character do? How does the character behave?


He doesn't really do much, he drinks, sleeps, sometimes brings Jamie to school early in the story, and then later on a bit later in the story, he treats his son and daughter much better than before, brings them to school often, accepts the facts and that a lot of things are going to be different, unavoidbly.

L - Looks


What does the character look like? How does the character dress?


He always wears some casual clothes at home everyone would wear, but just a bit dirtier than usual, also a bit worn out too and wears slippers usually. His looks drastically improves as his attitude and behaviour towards others also improves later on in the story.
